“Vocabulary:”



1. Captivating: holding your attention by being extremely interesting, exciting, pleasant, or attractive

2. Depict: to represent by or as if by a picture

3. Internalized: similar to embodied “demonstrate epitomize exemplify exhibit express illustrate incorporate manifest mirror personify realize stand for symbolize typify.”

4. Perceptions: the act or faculty of apprehending by means of the senses or of the mind; cognition; understanding.

5. Ruthless: having no pity
